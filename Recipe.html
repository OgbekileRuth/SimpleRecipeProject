PANCAKE

A fluffy, golden-brown breakfast treat made with a batter of flour, eggs, and milk, cooked on griddle and served with sweet or savory toppings.

reffered to as Diet in Nigeria, it contains protein(eggs), carbohydrate(Flour)and fats and oil(from the butter and vegetables)

Pancake Ingredients

  • 1 cup | 150g of plain flour
  • 2 medium eggs
  • 1/2 cup | 125 mls of evaporated milk(peak or carnation are common in Nigeria)
  • 2 tablespoons of margarine
  • 1 cup | 250 mls of water
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 6 teaspoons of sugar (or to taste)
  • Ground chilli pepper (to taste)
  • Vegetable oil

Step-by-Step Instruction

  1. Put the magarine in a small bowl and melt in the microwave. You can also place the bowl in a bigger bowl of hot water to melt the magarine.
  2. Break the two eggs into the melted margarine and beat till smooth. Then add 1/2 cup of water
  3. Add the flour and mix well till there are minimal lumps.
  4. Add the milk, salt, sugar, grounded pepper and the remaining 1/2 cup of water, stir well.
  5. Blend the mix using a liquid blender. This will give the pancake batter the smoothest consistency. If you don't want to blend it, no problem, just mix the ingredients very well so that the lumps are minimal
Note:You can also add onions to the pancake but make sure that your blender can liquefy the onions. If not, the pieces of onions will not let you achieve very thin pancakes.

Cooking Directions

  1. Set a frying pan over medium heat. Add a few drops of vegetable oil. Just enough to lubricate the frying pan.
  2. When hot, pour some pancake batter into the frying pan, Tilt the pan so that the batter coats the surface of the pan evenly. This should be a very thin coat.
  3. Fry the underside of the pancake for approximately 1 minute, shaking the pan from time to time till the pancake comes loose. You can also loosen it with the spatula. Check the underside to be sure it has browned to your liking, preferably light brown.
  4. Flip the pancake so you can fry the top side as well
  5. The pancake is done when both sides are light brown.

SPAGHETTI

A type of pasta made in the shape of long thin strings, Also classic Italian favorite, featuring long, slender strands of pasta tossed in a rich, tangy tomato sauce and topped with parmesan cheese.

  • Spaghetti: The quantity you can wrap between your thumb and index finger
  • Beef: 5-6 normal cuts
  • Vegetables: 500g
    • - Cabbage - Green pepper - Carrots - Green peas - Green beans
  • Tomato Stew: 600mls or 20fl. oz.
  • Pepper and salt (to taste)
  • Onions: 1 medium bulb
  • Seasoning - 2-3 stock cubes cubes & Thyme (1 teaspoon)

Step-by-Step Instruction

  1. Make some tomato stew if you do not have any.
  2. Break the spaghetti into short pieces. I usually break one strand of spaghetti into three pieces.
  3. Cut the beef into small pieces. It is best to buy the part of the beef that has streaks of fat in it. This greatly improves the taste of this recipe
  4. Wash and cut the cabbage, green pepper and green beans into small pieces. Also wash, scrape and cut the carrots into tiny pieces. Cut the onions into small pieces and remove the green peas from the pods. If you will use tinned green peas, simply drain the preservation water and rinse with some fresh water.

Tips for Cooking Spaghetti:

Spaghetti should be cooked with a very big pot. The pot should contain the spaghetti and still have plenty of unused volume. Spaghetti should also be cooked in plenty of water. The quantity of water should be at least double the quantity of the spaghetti. To cook spaghetti, first of all, boil water; add a few spoons of vegetable oil to the boiling water and add the spaghetti. Stir the contents very well and cover the pot. With some spaghetti, you need to stir till the water starts to boil again before covering the pot. The large amount of water, the boiling water and the vegetable oil altogether prevent the spaghetti strands from sticking together.

Cooking Directions

  1. Cook the beef with the onions, thyme and stock cubes (Maggi or Knorr).
  2. While the meat is cooking, start cooking the spaghetti in a generous amount of water
  3. When the beef is done, add the tomato stew. Add the vegetables, salt and pepper to taste and cook for about 5 minutes
  4. When the spaghetti is done to your personal preference, drain the water and pour the mixture of beef stock and tomato stew you prepared earlier into the pot of spaghetti. Stir well, cover and leave to stand for about 5 minutes.
  5. The Spaghetti is ready to be served.

Fruits Salad

A Mixture of raw or cold ingredients, typically vegetables, served with a dressing such as vinegar or mayonnaise. it also a fresh mix of cripsy greens,juicy vegetables, and savory toppings, all combined in a flavorful and healthy bowl.

Salad Ingredients

Fresh Fruits:
  1. 1 cup pineapple, diced
  2. 1 cup strawberries, hulled and sliced
  3. 1 cup grapes, halved
  4. 1 cup kiwi, peeled and diced
  5. 1 cup mango, diced
  6. 1 orange, peeled and segmented
Dressing:
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • Garnish:
  • Fresh mint leaves, chopped (optional)

Directions:

  1. Prepare the Fruit: Wash, peel, and chop all the fruits into bite-sized pieces.
  2. In a Large Bowl: Combine all the prepared fruits.
  3. Make the Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together honey, lime juice, and vanilla extract until well combined
  4. Pour the Dressing: Pour the dressing over the fruit mixture and toss gently to coat.
  5. Chill: Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  6. Garnish: Just before serving, garnish with chopped fresh mint leaves, if desired.
  7. Serve: Serve chilled and enjoy!

Tips for making salad:

  1. Use Fresh Fruits: Choose fresh and ripe fruits for the best flavor and texture
  2. Customize: Feel free to add or substitute your favorite fruits to make the salad your own.
  3. Adjust the Dressing: Taste and adjust the dressing to your liking, adding more honey for sweetness or lime juice for tartness
    4. Enjoy your delicious fruit salad!

Gizdodo

Gizdodo: A popular Nigerian dish prepared with a combination of gizzards and plantains, all fried to a crispy perfection and seasoned with spices.Gizdodo is also known as Gizzards and dodo, or Gizdodo, so if you see it on a buffet menu, don't be surprised, it's just a normal gizzards and plantain meal with a new name.

Gizdodo Ingredients

  • 500 grams, Chicken gizzards
  • 1 ripe Plantain
  • 3 large Roma tomatoes (fresh tomatoes)
  • 2 Habenero pepper (atarodo)(to suit your taste)
  • 1 red bell pepper (tatashe) (optional)
  • 1 teaspoonful ground Crayfish(optional)
  • 1 large onion bulb (cut into 2 halves)
  • 1 garlic clove/half teaspoon garlic powder(optional)
  • Half teaspoon each of Curry and Thyme
  • 2 bouillon/stock cubes
  • Vegetable oil (enough for frying)

Step-by-Step Instruction

  1. Clean the Gizzards by removing the yellow lining. Then wash the gizzards with salted water or lemon juice. Scrub well to get rid of any dirt. Then Rinse the gizzards in running water, strain and set aside. You can cut the cooked gizzards into smaller bite sizes.
    2. Tip: If you buy yours pre-packed, it is possible that the yellow lining won't be there, so simply wash in salted water or lime/lemon juice,rinse and strain.
  2. Place the cleaned gizzards into a pot; pour enough water to half the level of the gizzards. Season the gizzard with half onion bulb, garlic, one stock cube, thyme, curry and salt to taste. Cook until tender and set aside.
    3. Tip: Gizzard is a tough meat(a muscle meat to be precise) and most people assume that it does not absorb any of the seasonings while cooking. But i highly recommend that you season the gizzards properly using as much seasoning as you are comfortable with and let the stock dry as it cooks the meat. You'll be amazed at how tasty,gizzards can be!
  3. Next, Cut the plantains into cubes, just like it was done here and fry in hot vegetable oil until brown. Transfer onto a sieve.
Note: Chicken gizzards is best used for this recipe, but you can also substitute it for turkey or duck gizzards. As for the plantains, I'll highly recommend ripe plantains, but not overly ripe, so that you don't end up with a less appealing meal.

Cooking Directions

  1. Fry the gizzards in the same oil until brown and also transfer onto a sieve lined with paper towels to absorb excess oil.
    2. Tip: For a healthier option, grill the gizzards in the oven until brown.
  2. Chop the tomatoes and peppers and blend coarsely
  3. Heat up some oil, add chopped onions, fry a bit and add the ground crayfish (remember the crayfish is optional)
  4. Stir-Fry for 5 seconds and then pour the blended mix into the pot and fry for about 10-15 minutes or until the sour taste is lost.
  5. Then add one stock cube and salt to taste. Cook for 2 minutes. And set aside
    6. Tip: You'll know the sauce is ready when the oil rises to the top of the stew.
  6. Add the fried gizzards to the sauce and cook for i minute to soak in the sauce.

PLANTAIN MOSA

Plantain Mosa is overripe plantains mashed and mixed with other ingredients then deep-fried in vegetable oil. A traditional Nigerian dish made with ripe plantains, onions, and peppers, all mashed together and formed into crispy, golden-brown patty.

Plantain Mosa Ingredients

    The following ingredients give 16 medium balls of Plantain Mosa
  • 1 over-ripe plantain
  • 3 heaped tablespoons plain flour
  • 2 teaspoons fast action yeast
  • 8 table spoon warm water
  • Habenero pepper
  • Salt (to Taste)
  • Vegetable oil Vegetable oil (for frying)

More information about the ingredients

  1. The vegetable oil should be about 3 inches deep when poured into the pot.
  2. You can use cayenne pepper (fresh or dry) in the absence of Habanero pepper. The dry one needs to be ground into powder before use.

    3. Step-by-Step Instruction

    1. Mix the fast action yeast with the warm water making sure there are no lumps. Cover and set aside.
    2. Peel the overripe plantain and mash with a fork till a good blend is achieved. Set aside.
    3. Sift the plain flour and the salt into a sizeable bowl. Mix thoroughly.
    4. Add the yeast solution and stir till you get a medium consistency. If it is too thick, add a few more tablespoons of warm water and mix till you get a medium consistency.
    5. Add the mashed plantain. Mix with a spatula till well incorporated.
    6. Cover with aluminium foil. leave in a warm place to rest for 10-15 minutes.
    7. After that time, you'll notice that it a bit fluffy
    8. Beat the batter with a spoon then add the habanero pepper (cut into pieces)

    Cooking Directions

    1. Heat some vegetable oil in a pot. The oil should be about 3 inches deep. The oil is hot enough when a small quantity of batter dropped into the oil sizzles and quickly comes to the surface of the oil
    2. Scoop small amounts of the batter into the oil using your finger tips. Watch as they sizzle, come up to the surface of the oil and puff up.
    3. Stir constantly till golden. Reduce the heat if you think the plantain mosas are browning too quickly. This is to ensure that the inside is well cooked by the time the outside gets brown.
    4. When done, transfer to a paper-lined sieve to drain. And your Plantain Mosa is Ready.

    Bread Rolls

    A Small round piece of bread, Bread Rolls: Soft, fluffy, and freshly baked, these bread rolls are perfect for sopping up sauces or olive oil, or simply enjoying on their own.

    Bread Ingredients

      The following ingredients make 9 Breads
    • 1 1/2 cups warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
    • 1 tablespoon active dry yeast
    • 2 tablespoons white sugar
    • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 4 cups bread flour

    Step-by-Step Instruction

    1. In a large bowl, stir together warm water, yeast, and sugar. let stand until creamy, about 10 minutes.
    2. To the yeast mixture, add the oil, salt, and 2 cups flour. Stir in the remaining flour, 1/2 cup at a time, until the dough has pulled awayn from the sides of the bowl. Turn out onto a lightly floured surface, and knead until smooth and elastic, about 8 minutes, Lightly oil a large bowl, place the dough in the bowl, and turn to coat.Cover with a damp cloth, and let rise in a warm place until doubled in volume, about 30 mins.
    3. Deflate the dough, and turn it out onto a lightly floured surface. Divide the dough into 16 equal pieces, and form into round balls. Place on lightly greased baking sheets at least 2 inches apart. Cover the rolls with a damp cloth, and let rise until doubled in volume, about 30 minutes. Meanwhile,preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
    Note: Easy to make French bread rolls. Dough can be made in mixer, bread maker, or by hand. Loaves or rolls can be brushed before baking with a glaze of 1 beaten egg white mixed with 1 tablespoon water if desired.

    Cooking Directions

    • Bake for 18 to 20 minutes in the preheated oven, or until golden brown.
